I-ZOLBIN 2500

CComposition: Each bolus contains

Albendazole (2500 mg)

  • Information
Indications
I-Zolbin 2500 Bolus is a Broad Spectrum Anthelmintic. To Eliminate Round Worm, Lung Worms, Tape Worm and Liver Flukes in their imatire, Mature Stages with ovicidal activity.
Dosage
For the Eradication of Round worms, Lung Worms, Tape Worm and Liver Fluke:
Albendazole 7.5-10 mg per kg body weight.
Withdrawal times:

For Meat: 3 Days

For Milk: 14 Days
Precaution:
Do not use in the First 45 days of Pregnancy.
Store in a cool dry Place and Protect from light
Keep out of the reach of Children.

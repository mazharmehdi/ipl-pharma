SULF-B BOLUS

Composition:

Each Bolus contains
Sulphadimidine HCL 350mg

Indications

Suplhadimidine is highly effective in the treatment of enteric and respiratory infection in all species pyogenic infections, cocci, pasteurella, salmonella and coliform organisms.
Poultry:
Sulphadimidine is indicated in the treatment of coccidiosis and other poultry diseases, including infections like Coryza and Roup.

Precaution:

Do not use in the First 45 days of Pregnancy. Store in a cool dry Place and Protect from light Keep out of the reach of Children.

TREMOSULF

Composition:

Each bolus contains
Sulfadiazine 1000 mg,
Trimethoprim 200 mg

Indications

For the treatment of respiratory, intestinal and urinary diseases in cattle, calves, lambs, and horses.
Description: A wide spectrum antibiotic for the control and treatment of infectious diseases in large animals

Dosage

One bolus for 40 kg of body weight per day. Continue the treatment for 3 to 5 days.

Available Packing

Box of 5 blisters x 10 boluses
Shelf life:
2 years

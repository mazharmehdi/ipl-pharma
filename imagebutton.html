I-BENZAL 152

I-BENZAL 152

Compostion: Contains per bolus. Albendazole ……….152 mg

Indications
Prophylaxis and treatment of worm infections in cattle, calves, sheep and goats like: Gastrointestinal worms: Bunostomum, Cooperia, Chabertia, Haemonchus, Nematodirus, Oesophagostomum, Ostertagia, Strongyloides and Trichostrongylus spp. Lung worms: Dictyocaulus viviparus and D. filaria. Tapeworms: Monieza spp. Liver-fluke: Adult Fasciola hepatica
Dosage
For oral administration. Calves and cattle: 1 bolus per 30kg. Body weight. For liver-fluke: 1 bolus per 17 kg. Body weight. Sheep and goats: 1 bolus per 17 kg. Body weight. For liver-fluke: 1 bolus per 15 kg. Body weight
Available Packing
A50 boluses in a carton with blister
Withdrawal times:
For Meat: 12 Days For Milk: 14 Days
Contra indications/Side effects/Warning:
1-Administration in the first 45 days of gestation. 2-Hypersensitivity reactions. 3-Keep out of reach of children.
